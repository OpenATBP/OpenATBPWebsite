Open Beta 1.6

New Content

  • Added new game mode: 3v3 Bots! 3 players vs a team of 3 randomly selected bots.
  • Added 3 new bot-controlled champions: Ice King, Jake, and Lemongrab.
  • Improved bot AI. bots now make smarter decisions around ability usage and positioning.

Movement System Rework

  • Player hitbox now updates continuously throughout the entire movement ability process.
  • Movement abilities are now divided into three categories:
    • Dash — Cannot pass through obstacles.
    • Leap — Can jump over obstacles if the endpoint is outside them; otherwise the champion leaps to the nearest obstacle edge. Champions are immune to damage and most effects while airborne (see exceptions below).
    • Teleport — Behaves like Leap but is instant.

  • The following abilities still deal damage and apply debuffs to leaping champions:
    • Billy's Nothung (E)
    • BMO's BMO Is Camera (Q)
    • Choose Goose's Electric Charge (Q)
    • Cinnamon Bun's Ring of Frosting (E)
    • Flame Princess's Scatter Fire (Passive)
    • Ice King's Hailstorm's (W)

Movement Ability Categories

  • Billy's Ground Pound (W) — Leap
  • Choose Goose's Goose Leap (W) — Leap
  • Cinnamon Bun's Sticky Slide (W) — Leap
  • Finn's Bombastic Dash (W) — Dash
  • Fionna's Blade Rush (Q) — Dash
  • Flame Princess's Blazing Inferno (E) — Dash
  • Gunter's Belly Slide (Q) — Dash
  • The Lich's Well of Power (E) (second activation) — Teleport
  • Magic Man's Magic Away (E) — Dash
  • Peppermint Butler's You Rang? (W) — Leap
  • Princess Bubblegum's Cherry Boom (E) (second activation) — Leap
  • Sir Rattleballs' Shadowless Thrust (Q) — Leap (first activation), Dash (second activation)

BALANCE UPDATES

Champion Balance

Billy

  • Ground Pound (W)
    • Attack speed buff and move speed buff durations unified to 5 seconds.
    • Attack speed buff: 4s ➝ 5s
    • Move speed buff: 6s ➝ 5s

Finn

  • Armored Attack (Q)
    • move speed bonus changed from flat +0.5 to 10% move speed buff.

Jake

  • Stretchy Grab (Q)
    • Ability reworked into a pull
  • Giant Jake (E)
    • Activation now removes all negative effects. Jake is fully immune to all negative effects while his ultimate is active.

Peppermint Butler

  • Minty Aura (Q)
    • blind no longer persists longer than intended.

Item Balance

Anti-Magic Cube

  • [CHANGE] Power damage debuff now has a 6-second cooldown per target. Effect duration is 5 seconds.

Electrode Gun

  • [CHANGE] Passive reworked — abilities now grant a stack on champion hit (3s cooldown between stack gains). At 3 stacks, the next ability stuns the first champion hit for 0.5/1/1.5/2s. Stacks expire after 3 seconds.

Effect System Rework

  • All effects now stack correctly.

Bug Fixes & Improvements

  • Fixed an issue where only the first player to accept a party invite would successfully join the team. Subsequent accepted invites were incorrectly treated as declined.
  • Fixed incorrect buff durations on Goo Monster and Keeoth.
  • Fixed an issue where minions would move across the entire map to their correct position immediately after spawning.
  • Shortened announcer voice lines in the tutorial, reducing the time players are unable to move between prompts.

Economy & Progression

Coins earned and prestige points (percentage of next account level progress) per game mode have been updated:

Coins

  • Completing the tutorial for the first time: 1,000 coins
  • Winning a ranked game: 100 coins
  • Losing a ranked game: 75 coins
  • Winning 3v3 Bots: 40 coins
  • Losing 3v3 Bots: 20 coins
  • Winning Practice: 25 coins
  • Losing Practice: 10 coins

Prestige Points (Account XP % per match)

  • Winning a ranked game: 20%
  • Losing a ranked game: 10%
  • Winning 3v3 Bots: 10%
  • Losing 3v3 Bots: 5%
  • Winning Practice: 5%
  • Losing Practice: 2%
