Patch Notes

OpenATBP Update 1.5.1

Queue Changes

  • To make the experience better for all players and to create a better balance of teams, we have made adjustments to the queue system.
  • Premades have been disabled for the regular 3v3 queue to promote diversity in matchmaking, especially where there are few players online.
  • We have tweaked the queue dodging system to better detect when dodging is occurring and be less forgiving when players dodge. How is secret :3 pls don't dodge.
  • While alt accounts are not necessarily prohibited at this point, using alts to avoid a queue ban will result in complete erasure of all accounts created by the offender for the first offense, and an irreversible IP ban on the second offense.

Balance Updates

General Changes

  • Added around 10 armor / MR to each character's base stats to give everyone a bit more survivability.
  • Reworked how breach works. Your amount of breach is no longer a flat shred of the enemy's armor or MR, but rather takes a percentage. For example, 35 armor breach reduces 35% of the opponent's armor.
