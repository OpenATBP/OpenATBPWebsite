Patch Notes

OpenATBP Update 1.2

New Features

  • Added refresh button to client sidebar for easy refreshing, if needed.
  • Added default player icons to replace Lich icon for friends.
  • Added personal champion stat tracking.
  • [BETA] Added "Spicy Battle" functionality to BMO bot. Features are still being worked on.

Balance Updates

Billy

  • Lowered base HP from 550 to 500.
  • Lowered base attack damage from 65 to 50.
  • Increased base W cooldown by 2 seconds.
  • Lowered shield growth per level from 2 to 1.

Finn

  • Removed attack speed gained in ult.

Fionna

  • Power scaling on Q increased from 30% to 40%.

Flame Princess

  • Passive base damage decreased from 20 to 10.
  • Passive damage scaling increased from 35% to 45%.

Gunter

  • Increased base crit chance from 0% to 1%.
  • Increased crit damage from 200% to 250%.
  • Increased base armor breach from 0 to 5.

Hunson

  • Increased base life steal from 0% to 10%.
  • Increased base attack from 40 to 55.
  • Increased base armor from 10 to 15.
  • Hunson can now move slowly while in ult form.

Ice King

  • Increased speed from 2.65 to 2.8.
  • Decreased ult movement speed from 100% to 90%.
  • Deals 10% more damage to targets affected by CC.

Jake

  • Increased passive AD scaling from 40% to 60%.

Lemongrab

  • W's cooldown is now reduced by 30% if you only hit a non-champion.

The Lich

  • Reduced base attack damage from 40 to 35.

Lumpy Space Princess

  • Increased Q AP scaling from 40% to 70%.

NEPTR

  • Passive no longer disappears while standing in the bush.

Peppermint Butler

  • Passive now gives you up to 20% CDR for your W based on how long you stand still.
  • Ult now gives Pepp an increased 0.1 attack range.

Princess Bubblegum

  • PB turrets no longer scale on PB's health, but rather on PB's level. They now gain 50 HP per level.

Minions

  • Increased XP earned from 5 to 7.

Robo Suit

  • Decreased shields from 5/10/15/25 to 5/10/15/20.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed issue where BMO's W would not go on cooldown when stunned.
  • Fixed issue where Marceline's voice line would not play when transforming.
  • Fixed issue where minions were not receiving DC buff.
  • Fixed friends not showing party when a person leaves and visual bug with friends "denying" accepted invites.
  • Fixed visual bug where tower aggro would never go away.
  • Fixed visual bug where monster health was depleted when spawning.
  • Fixed issue where players could select the same champion.
  • Fixed how knockbacks are handled so that they do not pull when near walls.
  • Fixed an issue where Quinceanera LSP crashed the game.
