Open Beta 1.5.2

Champion balance

Flame Princess

  • Flame Blast (Q)
    • Increased hitbox of Fan of Flames to better match VFX
    • Decreased Fan of Flames damage from 120% to 50%

Peppermint Butler

  • You Rang? (W)
    • If used while Sense Evil is active, stun duration is increased to 2 seconds
  • Sense Evil (E)
    • Pep can now use You Rang? (W) while Sense Evil is active
    • Bonus speed decreased from 40% to 25%
    • Attack range boost decreased from 0.6 to 0.5

Bug Fixes & Improvements

  • Fixed a visual issue where, after resetting backpacks, other players would still see pre‑reset item upgrades
  • Removed the legacy passive from Grob Gob Glob Grod’s Device so it no longer triggers both old and new passives
  • Corrected Demon Blood Sword’s experience bonus, changing it from an unintended 15% back to the intended 10%
  • Updated the Demon Blood Sword’s buff description for owl/gnome kills to accurately reflect its new, reduced stat bonuses
