Open Beta 1.5

New Content

  • New Champion: Choose Goose
  • New Skin: Shadow Finn

New Game Mode: ARAM (3vs3)

  • Played on the Candy Streets (Practice) map.
  • Everyone is assigned a random champion.
  • Jungle camps spawn random monsters (including Kee-Oth and Goo Monster) that despawn and swap out if left at full HP for too long.
  • Health Pack spawn rate: 30 seconds.

New item: Sai

  • Replaces Me-Mow’s Dagger in Choose Goose’s Lootbox
  • Attack Damage: 10/25/50/75
  • Crit Chance: 5%/10%/15%/20%
  • Passive: Basic attacks have a chance to reduce the target’s Armor. The chance and amount of reduction are equal to your Crit Chance. This effect lasts 3 seconds and does not stack.

New item: Hotdog Knight’s Helmet

  • Replaces: Medal of bravery in Assassin's Starter-Kit
  • Armor: 10/15/17/20
  • Crit chance: 5%/10%/15%/30%
  • Armor Breach: 5/10/20/40

Icon Shop Tab

A new “Icon Shop” has been added to the OpenATBP client. You open it by clicking the three-line button in the top bar (next to your “Logged in as…” text) which reveals the menu with Icon Shop.

On the icon Shop page players can:

  • Browse all available profile icons
  • Purchase or equip icons
  • View unlock requirements

There are four rarity tiers:

  • Common: Can be purchased for 200 coins each
  • Blooby: Unlocked by completing specific achievements
  • Algebraic: Unlocked by completing more challenging achievements
  • Legendary: Exclusive rewards from special events or community milestones

Blooby Icons (Unlocked by achievement):

  • Cake - Get a Triple KO
  • Ignition Point - Reach the Master of Battle rank (at least 75 elo required)
  • Lord of Evil - Reach player level 25
  • Marshall Lee - Play 150 Ranked Matches
  • Me-Mow - Score 500 total KOs in Ranked matches
  • Rage - Achieve a 9-KO spree in a single Ranked match
  • Young Billy - Reach player level 30

Algebraic Icons (Unlocked by achievement):

  • Ancient Evil - Reach Immortal Warrior rank (at least 200 elo required)
  • Denial of Light - Score 1000 total KOs in Ranked matches
  • Ice Queen - Achieve a 15-KO spree in a single Ranked match
  • Phoebe - Reach player level 50
  • Simon - Play 500 Ranked matches
  • Tops Blooby - Get a Quad KO

Legendary Icons (Unlocked by special event):

  • Radiant Victory - Win the Community Highlight Contest

Balance Changes

Ability interruption

  • Gunter's Q, Peppermint Butler's W and Magic Man’s E can now be interrupted by silence effects.

Basic attack adjustments

Cinnamon Bun, Flame Princess and Magic Man:

  • Abilities now trigger a basic attack reset with a 0.5 seconds delay.
  • This helps to avoid ‘double attacks’ within a single animation.
  • Magic Man's Q basic attack reset now activates immediately on ability cast.

Bug Fixes & Improvements

  • Fixed an issue where exceeding the maximum elo bracket (going beyond the purple rank) would incorrectly assign the bronze rank icon ('burple'). Rank icons now correctly cap at the purple icon.
  • Fixed a bug where having the exact elo required to enter a new tier could result in the ‘tower’ error icon.
  • Fixed a bug where it was possible to create multiple accounts with the same display name.
  • Added safety checks to prevent certain abilities from being cast if the champion is dead.
  • Added error messages on the registration screen to inform users when registration fails.
  • Forgot Password screen now displays error messages when password reset fails.
  • Fixed Peppermint Butler's W not being interruptible.
  • Fixed Flame Princess's passive not dealing damage when crowd controlled.
  • Fixed Flame Princess’s passive not activating when dealing damage with her W and E.
  • Fixed Marceline's W not updating speed in the statistics menu.
  • Fixed Magic Man’s W voice line playing from the wrong source.
  • Added Hanson's W missing sound effect.
  • Resolved an issue with the Gumball Guardians' spawn behavior.
  • Fixed Lumpy Space Princess’s Q VFX firing with the wrong rotation. LSP can no longer auto-attack during Q cast time, preventing facing the wrong direction and incorrect VFX rotation.
  • Fixed an issue where if interrupted by a polymorph, LSP would not transform into a flambit. Now she correctly transforms.
  • If interrupted, LSP now immediately stops the E ability animation instead of continuing it.
  • The interruption sound effect now plays immediately when LSP E is interrupted, instead of when it was supposed to fire.
  • Fixed an issue with Practice Map fountains not healing correctly across the entire fountain area.
  • Disabled Finn’s W reset voice line from playing while he is dead.
  • Added a visual effect to Rattlebrain's E ability.
  • Updated Rattlebrain's E sound effects to more accurately reflect the original game.
  • Updated multiple in-game descriptions.
  • Fixed an issue where it was possible to perform actions after the game had ended in Practice Mode and Tutorial.
  • Fixed an issue where health packs and jungle monsters were not spawning at the correct times, according to the match timer.
  • Fixed an issue where the end-game summary screen would visually display coin and account level gains for non-ranked matches (these were not actually earned). The summary now correctly shows 0 coins and 0 XP gained for non-ranked modes.
  • Fixed a bug where the Robo Suit's movement speed boost was not removed upon taking damage, causing the speed boost to persist permanently.
  • Fixed multiple bugs affecting the Lich Hand item.
  • Fixed a bug where having two teammates with Simon Petrov's Glasses could cause infinite glasses buff spam on a third teammate without glasses, eventually freezing their game when both glasses bags were nearby.
  • Fixed an issue where bushes on the practice map did not grant invisibility. All bushes now properly hide champions from enemy vision.
