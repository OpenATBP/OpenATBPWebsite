Patch Notes

OpenATBP Update 1.1

New Features

  • Added new BMO skin - Olive & BMO! Thanks to CakeLancelot and Tom The Human.
  • Added Skill Based Match Making [BETA] when player capacity is above 28 players.
  • You can now attack and kill the practice bot.

Balance Updates

Billy

  • Reduced base HP from 600 to 550.
  • Reduced W base damage from 65 to 60.
  • Reduced W damage ratio from 50% to 40%.

Cinnamon Bun

  • Reduced Cinnamon Bun's ult damage ratio from 40% to 35%.

Gunter

  • Increased Gunter's ult base damage from 40 to 60.
  • Gunter can now cancel his ult by dashing with his Q.

Hunson

  • Increased Hunson's base HP from 425 to 475.
  • Hunson no longer stops moving after casting his W.

The Lich

  • Lich's Skully's speed has been reduced from 2.95 to 2.75.
  • Lich's Skully will now reset to Lich if he wanders too far.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed issue where Marceline would not lose movement speed after using her W in beast form.
  • Fixed issue where Rattleballs could reactivate his Q multiple times.
  • Fixed issue where death push/pull abilities caused weird champion movement.
