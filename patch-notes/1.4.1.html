Patch Notes

OpenATBP Update 1.4.1

Balance Updates

General Changes

  • Reduced max defenses allowed from 80 Armor / 70 MR to 65 Armor / 65 MR.
  • Reduced everyone's crit damage from 200% to 125%, with the exception of Gunter at 150%. Crit items have a higher chance to balance this change.

Item Changes

  • Night Sword: [NERF] Reduced AD from 25/50/75/125 to 10/25/55/110.
  • AX Bass: [BUFF] Increased AD from 25/50/75/125 to 35/55/100/150.
  • AX Bass: [ADDITION] Now heals for 15% of max HP on champion KP.
  • Lich Hand: [BUFF] Increased AP from 15/25/50/100 to 20/45/90/165.
  • Crystal Gem Apple: [NERF] Decreased AP from 10/20/30/50 to 5/10/20/35.
  • Magic Carpet: [BUFF] Increased speed from .05/.10/.15/.20 to .05/.10/.20/.40.
  • Fight King's Sword: [BUFF] Increased AD from 5/10/25/50 to 10/25/50/80.
  • Cosmic Gauntlet: [BUFF] Increased AP from 15/30/45/75 to 15/40/75/115.
  • Magic Nail: [BUFF] Increased max AD stacks per level from 25 to 40.
  • Lightning Sword: [BUFF] Increased max AP stacks per level from 35 to 55.
  • Glasses of Nerdicon: [BUFF] Increased XP passive from 10%/20%/25%/30% to 15%/25%/35%/35%.
  • Glasses of Nerdicon: [ADDITION] Maxing out this item now grants +1 spell point.
  • Demon Blood Sword: [CHANGE] Gnomes and Owls give +25 AD and +50 PD. Hugwolf and Grass Bear give +5 armor and +5 shield.
  • Demon Blood Sword: [BUFF] Buff duration increased from 30 seconds to 60 seconds.
  • Medal of Bravery: [NERF] Reduced attack speed from 75/150/250/350 to 75/125/200/275.
  • Medal of Bravery: [BUFF] Increased crit chance from 5%/7%/10%/15% to 5%/10%/15%/30%.
  • Enchiridion: [BUFF] Increased HP from 25/75/100/150 to 50/100/175/300.
  • Enchiridion: [CHANGE] Changed 75/125/200/275 attack speed to 5/10/15/25 AD.
  • Enchiridion: [NERF] Decreased crit chance from 3%/5%/7%/10% to 1%/2%/4%/8%.
  • Flame Cloak: [NERF] Reduced damage from 5% max HP to 3.5% max HP.
  • Me-Mow's Dagger: [BUFF] Increased crit chance from 5%/10%/15%/20% to 5%/15%/30%/60%.
  • Art of Fridjitsu: [BUFF] Increased crit chance from 3%/5%/7%/10% to 5%/10%/15%/20%.
