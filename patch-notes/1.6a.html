Patch Notes

OpenATBP Update 1.6

New Content

  • Added new game mode: 3v3 Bots. 3 players vs a team of 3 randomly selected bots.
  • Added 3 new bot-controlled champions: Ice King, Jake, and Lemongrab.

New User Icons

The Lich

  • Rarity: Common.
  • Unlock: 200 coins.

Bonnie

  • Rarity: Blooby.
  • Unlock: Earn 500 points in a single Ranked match.

Supportive Jake

  • Rarity: Blooby.
  • Unlock: Earn 250 total assists in Ranked matches.

Reaper

  • Rarity: Algebraic.
  • Unlock: Achieve a 25-KO spree in a single Ranked match.

Huntress Wizard

  • Rarity: Algebraic.
  • Unlock: Reach account level 99.

New Sounds

  • Added new voice lines and sound effects for champions.
  • Voice lines for Q and W abilities now have a 50% chance of playing.

Movement System Rework

  • Player hitbox now updates continuously for all movement states.
  • Dash: Cannot pass through obstacles.
  • Leap: Can jump over obstacles if the endpoint is outside them; otherwise the champion leaps to the nearest obstacle edge. Champions are immune to damage and most effects while airborne, with some exceptions.
  • Teleport: Behaves like Leap but is instant.
  • The following abilities still deal damage and apply debuffs to leaping champions: Billy's Nothung (E), BMO's Game Frame (W), Choose Goose's Electric Charge (Q), Cinnamon Bun's Ring of Frosting (E), Flame Princess's Scatter Fire (Passive), and Ice King's Hailstorm (W).

Movement Ability Categories

  • Billy's Ground Pound (W): Leap.
  • Choose Goose's Goose Leap (W): Leap.
  • Cinnamon Bun's Sticky Slide (W): Leap.
  • Finn's Bombastic Dash (W): Dash.
  • Fionna's Blade Rush (Q): Dash.
  • Flame Princess's Blazing Inferno (E): Dash.
  • Gunter's Belly Slide (Q): Dash.
  • The Lich's Well of Power (E), second activation: Teleport.
  • Magic Man's EAT IT! (W): Teleport.
  • Magic Man's Magic Away (E): Dash.
  • Peppermint Butler's You Rang? (W): Leap.
  • Princess Bubblegum's Cherry Boom (E), second activation on self: Leap.
  • Sir Rattleballs' Shadowless Thrust (Q): Leap on first activation, Dash on second activation.

Champion Balance

Billy

  • Ground Pound (W): Attack speed buff and move speed buff durations unified to 5 seconds.
  • Ground Pound (W): Attack speed buff duration increased from 4s to 5s.
  • Ground Pound (W): Move speed buff duration reduced from 6s to 5s.
  • Nothung (E): The ability can now be cast with variable range. Previously, Nothung could only be cast at maximum range; it can now be cast anywhere within a 6-unit radius.

Finn

  • Armored Attack (Q): Move speed bonus changed from flat +0.5 to 10% move speed buff.
  • Armored Attack (Q): Basic attack reset on ability activation now sets attack speed to 0.5s instead of 0, bringing it in line with all other champions' basic attack resets.

Jake

  • Stretchy Grab (Q): Ability reworked into a pull.
  • Stretchy Grab (Q): Max ability range decreased from 9 to 7 units to match the stretch animation. Ability hitbox width remains constant across the skillshot's duration.
  • Giant Jake (E): Activation now removes all negative effects. Jake is fully immune to all negative effects while his ultimate is active.

Lumpy Space Princess

  • Hot Bod (W): Increased ability duration from 3.5s to 4s.

Marceline

  • Blood Mist/Hunter (W): Basic attack reset on ability activation now sets attack speed to 0.5s instead of 0, bringing it in line with all other champions' basic attack resets.
  • Transform (E): Removed unintended basic attack reset.

Peppermint Butler

  • Minty Aura (Q): Blind no longer persists longer than intended.

Sir Rattleballs

  • The Sword Stuff (E): Polymorph previously cancelled this ability; it now only disables it for the duration of the polymorph.

Item Balance

Anti-Magic Cube

  • Power damage debuff now has a 6-second cooldown per target. Effect duration is 5 seconds.

Electrode Gun

  • Passive reworked. Abilities now grant a stack on champion hit, with a 3s cooldown between stack gains. At 3 stacks, the next ability stuns the first champion hit for 0.5/1/1.5/2s. Stacks expire after 3 seconds.

Effect System Rework

  • All effects now stack correctly. Multiple instances of the same effect compound as expected regardless of whether the effect operates on a percentage or a flat value.

Polymorph Update

  • Polymorph now disables Flame Princess's Blazing Inferno (E) for its duration.
  • Polymorph now disables The Lich's Trail of Death (Q) for its duration.
  • Polymorph now disables Lumpy Space Princess's Hot Bod (W) for its duration.
  • Polymorph now disables Marceline's Blood Mist/Hunter (W) in Vampire Form for its duration.
  • Polymorph now disables Peppermint Butler's Minty Aura (Q) for its duration.
  • Polymorph now disables Sir Rattleballs' The Sword Stuff (E) for its duration.

Bug Fixes & Improvements

  • Bots now correctly display in the TAB view and end game summary.
  • Disconnect buffs now apply in bot games.
  • Fixed an issue where bots would not be revealed from stealth when damaged or when attacking.
  • Improved bot AI. Bots now make smarter decisions around ability usage and positioning.
  • Improved hit detection for all projectiles. Projectile hit detection now uses a rectangle that matches the spell indicator shape.
  • Fixed an issue where skillshots that destroy on first affected target would not hit the closest one.
  • Improved minion lane pathing, targeting, and wave stacking. Fixed issues where minions would get stuck, stop moving, or stand still in lane instead of pushing.
  • Fixed an issue where only the first player to accept a party invite would successfully join the team. Subsequent accepted invites were incorrectly treated as declined.
  • Fixed incorrect buff durations on Goo Monster and Keeoth.
  • Fixed an issue where minions would move across the entire map to their correct position immediately after spawning.
  • Shortened announcer voice lines in the tutorial, reducing the time players are unable to move between prompts.
  • Added a status icon to indicate when a champion is affected by Anti-Magic Cube's special effect.
  • Corrected Ice King's Q projectile range to match the cast indicator.

  • Winning a ranked game: 100 coins.
  • Losing a ranked game: 75 coins.
  • Winning 3v3 Bots: 40 coins.
  • Losing 3v3 Bots: 20 coins.
  • Winning Practice: 25 coins.
  • Losing Practice: 10 coins.
  • Winning a ranked game: 40% account experience.
  • Losing a ranked game: 20% account experience.
  • Winning 3v3 Bots: 20% account experience.
  • Losing 3v3 Bots: 10% account experience.
  • Winning Practice: 10% account experience.
  • Losing Practice: 5% account experience.
