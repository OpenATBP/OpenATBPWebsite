Player Controls and Actions

In OpenATBP, controlling your champion is simple, but good timing and positioning make a huge difference.

Left-click ground Move your champion to the clicked location.
Left-click enemy Attack the selected target.
Right-click ground Send a ping to alert your allies.

Champion Abilities

Each champion has three active abilities mapped to Q, W, and E. These abilities can deal damage, apply debuffs, create zones, move your champion, or provide defensive effects.

  • Every ability has its own cooldown timer.
  • Once an ability is used, you must wait for it to become available again.
  • OpenATBP does not use mana, so abilities are only limited by cooldowns.

Passive Powers

Every champion also has a passive ability. Passives are always active or automatically trigger when their conditions are met.

Damage and Death

Every unit, except Magic Man's decoy, has Health Points. When a unit's HP reaches zero, it is knocked out.

Champion Knockouts and Respawning

When a champion is KO'd, they respawn at their team's base after a delay. The respawn timer increases as the match progresses.

Base respawn 10 seconds
Scaling +1 second for every full minute elapsed
Example At 5 minutes, respawn time is 15 seconds

Gaining Experience and Leveling Up

Defeating enemy champions, minions, and jungle creeps grants Experience Points. Earning enough XP causes your champion to level up.

Leveling Benefits

  • Your champion's base stats increase.
  • You gain a Skill Point.
  • Skill Points can be spent on Battle Junk upgrades from your backpack.

Battle Junk Upgrades

Backpack items can improve many stats, depending on the selected loadout.

Offense

  • Critical Chance

Defense

Utility

Combat System

In OpenATBP, damage is split into two main types. Understanding the difference helps you choose upgrades and counter enemy champions.

Attack Damage

Physical damage usually dealt by basic attacks and some abilities.

  • Minions
  • Jungle monsters
  • Towers
  • Some champion abilities

Power Damage

Magical or special ability-based damage.

  • Most champion abilities
  • Jungle boss special attacks

Disconnect Buffs

To help balance uneven matches caused by disconnects, OpenATBP gives stat boosts to players left behind when their teammates go offline.

These buffs only apply in real matches, not PvP test matches. If both teams have disconnected players, the buffs cancel each other out.

One teammate disconnected

  • +20% Armor
  • +20% Shields
  • +15% Movement Speed

Two teammates disconnected

  • All previous buffs
  • +20% Attack Damage
  • +20% Power Damage

Full team disconnected

The disconnected team automatically forfeits the match.

Crowd Control Effects

Crowd Control, often shortened to CC, refers to effects that restrict movement, attacks, abilities, or control over your champion.

Effect What it does
Root You cannot move or use movement abilities, but you can still attack and use non-movement abilities.
Stun You cannot move, attack, or use abilities.
Charm You lose control and automatically walk toward the charmer. You cannot attack or use abilities.
Slow Your movement speed is reduced, but you can still attack and cast abilities.
Fear You lose control, run in a random direction, then freeze until the effect ends. You cannot use abilities.
Silence You cannot cast abilities, but you can still move and use basic attacks.
Blind Your basic attacks deal no damage. Movement and abilities are unaffected.
Polymorph You are transformed into a Flambit. You cannot use abilities or basic attacks, and you damage nearby teammates.
Airborne You are knocked back or pulled. You cannot move or use abilities while airborne.

Altars

Altars are control points that provide temporary team buffs. Securing them can swing a fight or help your team push objectives.

Mid Altar

The most contested altar. Capturing it grants the team +25% Attack Damage and Power Damage.

Side Altars

Side Altars grant +25% Armor and Shields for a limited time.

Re-Capture

After an altar is captured, it becomes available again after 1.5 minutes.

Altar Capture Speed

  • If your team has 1 more player on the altar than the enemy team, capture takes 5 seconds.
  • If your team has 2 or more additional players, Fast Capture Mode activates and capture takes 2 seconds.

Goo Buff Synergy

Players with the Goo Buff amplify altar effects by 50%.

  • Mid Altar becomes +37.5% Attack Damage and Power Damage.
  • Side Altars become +37.5% Armor and Shields.
  • If a player has 0 Armor or Shields, Side Altar buffs grant at least +5 to each stat.

Health Packs

Health Packs are valuable pickups placed around the battlefield. They can keep you alive during long fights or help you recover before contesting an objective.

Instant heal Restores 15% maximum HP
Regeneration buff +15 Health Regeneration for 1 minute
Initial spawn 14:00
Respawn 60 seconds after pickup

Strategic Use

  • Health Packs are limited, so track their locations and respawn times.
  • They are especially useful before altar fights, jungle contests, or tower pushes.
