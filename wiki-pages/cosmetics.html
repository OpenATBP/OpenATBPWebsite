Welcome to the Cosmetics Hub!

Here in OpenATBP, you can personalize your experience with a variety of awesome cosmetic upgrades! While Coins are a key currency, some of the most prestigious items are earned through dedication and skill.

How to Earn Coins

You can earn Coins just by playing the game! Here's how it works:

  • Win a Ranked Match (3v3): +100 Coins
  • Lose a Ranked Match (3v3): +80 Coins
  • Complete the Tutorial (first time only): +700 Coins

Ranked matches can be entered via the 3vs3 Quick Match button or by using the Invite Friends option to team up.

No matter how the match ends, you're always earning progress!

User Profile Icons

Personalize your profile with unique icons! You can browse all available icons below. To acquire them, visit the "Icon Shop" in the OpenATBP client. Access it by clicking the three-line menu button in the top bar (next to your "Logged in as..." text) and selecting "Icon Shop."

Icons are categorized into four rarity tiers:

  • Common: Can be purchased for 200 coins each.
  • Blooby: Unlocked by completing specific achievements.
  • Algebraic: Unlocked by completing more difficult achievements.
  • Legendary: Exclusive rewards from special events or community milestones.

Hover over an icon below to see its rarity and how to unlock it!
