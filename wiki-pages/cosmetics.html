How to Earn Coins

You can earn Coins just by playing the game. Ranked matches can be entered through the 3vs3 Quick Match button or by using Invite Friends to team up.

  • Win a Ranked Match (3v3): +100 Coins
  • Lose a Ranked Match (3v3): +80 Coins
  • Complete the Tutorial for the first time: +700 Coins

No matter how the match ends, you are always earning progress.

User Profile Icons

Personalize your profile with unique icons. To get them, visit the Icon Shop in the OpenATBP client by clicking the three-line menu button in the top bar and selecting Icon Shop.

  • Common: Purchased for 200 Coins each.
  • Blooby: Unlocked by completing specific achievements.
  • Algebraic: Unlocked by completing more difficult achievements.
  • Legendary: Exclusive rewards from special events or community milestones.
