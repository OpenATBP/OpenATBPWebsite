Ranked System

Play Ranked Matches to climb through OpenATBP's rank ladder. Your rank is based on your current Elo.

How to Gain Rank

You can increase your rank by playing Ranked Matches through:

  • The 3vs3 Quick Match button
  • Inviting friends to a ranked lobby

Elo System

  • +1 Elo for each win or tie
  • No Elo gain on a loss
  • Elo can only be lost by dodging a match or leaving mid-game

Matchmaking

Matchmaking is completely randomized. There is no skill-based matchmaking, so every match is a wild card.
