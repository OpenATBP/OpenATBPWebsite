How Backpacks Work

Item Sets

Each backpack contains a collection of in-game items that directly empower your chosen champion. Most backpacks include five distinct items focused around a specific build style.

Match Progression

As players level up during a match, they gain points that can be allocated into backpack items to increase stats such as damage, speed, survivability, or utility.

Strategic Choices

A maximum of four points can be invested into a single item, encouraging players to balance their upgrades instead of maxing everything at once.
