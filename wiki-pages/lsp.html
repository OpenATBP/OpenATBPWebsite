Lumpy Space Princess
Princess of Lumpy Space.
Release date 19-03-2014
Attack range type Ranged
Roles Wizard

Abilities

My Lumps (Passive)

LSP's basic attacks add Lumps (10 max). Lumps are consumed by Buuuumps to heal 3% of her max Health health for each Lump she has accumulated, up to 30%.

Cooldown: none

Buuuumps (Q)

LSP fires a lumpy beam blast that damages (10 + 70% Power damage power damage) and fears all enemies it hits for 2 seconds. Buuuumps also heals LSP for a percentage of her current maximum Health health based on My Lumps (passive).

Cooldown: 15s (12s at level 10)

Hot Bod (W)

LSP forms a super hot cloud around her bod that moves with her and damages (20 + 50% Power damage power damage / sec) enemies caught inside.

Cooldown: 12s (7s at level 10)

Drama Bomb (E)

LSP winds up and throws her phone across the entire map. It hurts enemies and heals allies in its path (250 + 40% Power damage power damage), the amount is reduced by 30% on each subsequent target.

Cooldown: 75s (30s at level 10)
