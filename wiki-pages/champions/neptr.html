Abilities

Hide 'n Seek Passive

Entering Brush gives NEPTR +35% speed and +25% attack speed for 3.5 seconds.

Cooldown: none

BoomMerringue Pie Q

NEPTR throws a projectile that passes through enemies, dealing 35 + 60% power damage. Damage decreases for each enemy hit.

The projectile pauses at max range, resets its damage reduction, and returns to NEPTR, dealing 35 + 60% power damage again.

Cooldown: 10s, 6s at level 10

Shoofly Pie Mines W

Places a pie mine at the target location. When an enemy comes too close, the mine activates and explodes after 0.5 seconds, dealing area damage: 25 + 65% power damage.

Affected enemies are slowed by 40% for 3 seconds. Up to 3 mines can be active at a time.

Cooldown: 10s, 5s at level 10

Pie to the Face E

Short range AoE that knocks back and silences foes for 1 second. It also deals damage over time: 40 + 30% power damage per second for 3 seconds.

Cooldown: 75s, 42s at level 10
