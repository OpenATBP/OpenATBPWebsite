NEPTR
The Never-Ending Pie-Throwing Robot created by Finn.
Release date 2015-04-13
Attack range type Ranged
Roles Wizard, Jinxer

Abilities

Hide 'n Seek (Passive)

Entering Brush gives NEPTR +35% speed and +25% attack speed for 3.5 seconds.

Cooldown: none

BoomMerringue Pie (Q)

NEPTR throws a projectile that passes through enemies doing damage (35 + 60% power damage) that decreases for each enemy hit. Pauses at max range, resets damage reduction and does (35 + 60% power damage) returning to NEPTR.

Cooldown: 10s (6s at level 10)

Shoofly Pie Mines (W)

Places a pie mine at target location until an enemy gets too close the mine activates and after a brief delay, explodes causing area damage (75 + 40% power damage) and slows by 40% for 3 seconds. Can have up to 3 mines placed at any one time.

Cooldown: 10s (5s at level 10)

Pie to the Face (E)

Short range AoE that will knockback and silence foes for 1 seconds and do damage over time (40 + 40% power damage) per second for 3 seconds.

Cooldown: 75s (42s at level 10)
