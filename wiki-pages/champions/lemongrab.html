Abilities

UNACCEPTABLE! Passive

When Lemongrab is hit by an enemy, he gains a level of UNACCEPTABLE!.

Each level increases the damage and stun time of DUNGEON! by 10% decreases the cast delay of the ability. Lemongrab can have up to 3 stacks. Each passive stack increases Lemongrab's shields by 5.

Lemongrab can only gain a passive level every 2 seconds and loses a level after 6 seconds of not being hit by a power.

Cooldown: none

Sound Sword Q

Lemongrab's magical Sound Sword emits a cone of pure noise, damaging enemies for 10 + 25% power damage and slowing them by 40% for 2.5 seconds.

Cooldown: 10s, 8s at level 10

My Juice W

Lemongrab drops the Lemon Bomb. After a brief 1.25 second delay, a massive lemon drops on the targeted area, dealing 10 + 50% power damage and blinding enemies for 4 seconds.

Enemies caught at the epicenter take 25% additional damage and are also silenced for 2 seconds.

My Juice's cooldown is decreased by 30% if Lemongrab does not hit any champion with the ability.

Cooldown: 12s, 10s at level 10

Dungeon! E

Lemongrab summons Lemon Dungeon Cells that damage enemies for 100 + 30% power damage and stun them for 2 seconds in the target area.

Damage and duration are increased by 10% times Lemongrab's current level of UNACCEPTABLE!.

The delay before the ability takes effect starts at 1.25 seconds with 0 stacks of UNACCEPTABLE!, and decreases by 0.25 seconds for each stack, reaching a minimum of 0.5 seconds at 3 stacks.

Cooldown: 60s, 47s at level 10
