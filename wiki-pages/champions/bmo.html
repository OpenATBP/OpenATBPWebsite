Abilities

Slow-MO Passive

After using 3 Powers, Beemo's next ability slows any enemies hit by 50% for 2.5 seconds.

Cooldown: none

BMO Is Camera Q

Beemo projects a powerful light beam that damages 40 + 30% power damage and blinds targets for 2.5 seconds.

Cooldown: 12s, 8s at level 10

Game Frame W

Beemo creates a ring of video game energy that increases his armor by 20% and shields by 50% while periodically blasting enemies in its area, dealing 10 + 40% power damage per second for 3 seconds.

When the power ends, a huge burst stuns opponents for 1 second in the area and deals additional damage based on how long the power was running. The bonus damage increases by 10% per second, up to a maximum of 30% at 3 seconds. Beemo can use W again to end the power early.

Cooldown: 16s, 11s at level 10

Bouncy Bee E

Beemo unleashes the Bouncy Bee: a huge, slow-moving projectile that crushes opponents in its path. It can hit multiple targets, dealing 110 + 50% power damage to the first enemy and 15% less to each subsequent target hit.

Cooldown: 50s, 37.5s at level 10
