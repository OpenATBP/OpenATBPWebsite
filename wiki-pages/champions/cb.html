Abilities

Perfect Sandwich Passive

Using a power heals Cinnamon Bun for 7.5% of his total Health.

Cooldown: none

Lance Strike Q

A lance strike in a medium sized area near Cinnamon Bun that does 30 + 60% power damage. This is a quick, low cooldown power. Resets auto-attack timer.

Cooldown: 6.5s, 4.5s at level 10

Sticky Slide W

Cinnamon Bun dashes, creating a trail of sticky frosting for 5 seconds. The frosting damages enemies for 10 + 30% power damage per second and slows enemies by 20% for 1 second.

Cooldown: 15s, 12s at level 10

Ring of Frosting E

Cinnamon Bun can place up to 2 rings that deal 50 + 60% power damage per-second AoE damage, with a final burst after 4.5 seconds or by pressing E again.

These 2 rings can be placed on top of one another to make a larger ring with 2x range. Small rings deal 25% more damage. While Cinnamon Bun is standing inside a ring, he gains a 10% buff to movement speed.

Cooldown: 60s, 47s at level 10
