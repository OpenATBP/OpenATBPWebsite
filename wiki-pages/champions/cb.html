Cinnamon Bun
The guardian of Flame Princess.
Release date 2015-04-13
Attack range type Melee
Roles Fighter, Trapper
Statistics per level

Abilities

Perfect Sandwich (Passive)

Using a power heals CB for 5% of his total Health.

Cooldown: none

Lance Strike (Q)

A lance strike in a medium sized area near CB that does (30 + 60% power damage). This is a quick, low cooldown power. Resets auto-attack timer.

Cooldown: 6.5s (4.5s at level 10)

Sticky Slide (W)

Cinnamon Bun Dashes, creating a trail of sticky frosting for 5 seconds. The frosting damages (10 + 30% power damage) per second and slows enemies by 20% for 1 second.

Cooldown: 17s (14s at level 10)

Ring of Frosting (E)

Cinnamon Bun can place up to 2 rings that do (50 + 35% power damage) per-second AoE damage, with a final burst after 4.5 seconds or can be activated by a final press of E. These 2 rings can be placed on top of one another to make a larger ring with 2x range. Small rings deal 50% more damage. While Cinnamon Bun is standing inside a ring, he gains a 10% buff to movement speed.

Cooldown: 55s (42s at level 10)
