Cinnamon Bun
The guardian of Flame Princess.
Release date 2015-04-13
Attack range type Melee
Roles Fighter, Trapper

Abilities

Perfect Sandwich (Passive)

Using a power heals CB for 5% of his total Health.

Cooldown: none

Lance Strike (Q)

A lance strike in a medium sized area near CB that does (30 + 60% power damage). This is a quick, low cooldown power. Resets auto-attack timer.

Cooldown: 6.5s (4.5s at level 10)

Sticky Slide (W)

Cinnamon Bun Dashes, creating a trail of sticky frosting for 5 seconds. The frosting damages (10 + 30% power damage / sec) and slows 20 + (1*level)% for 2 + (0.1 * level) seconds.

Cooldown: 17s (14s at level 10)

Ring of Frosting (E)

Cinnamon Bun place up to 2 rings that do (50 + 35% power damage) per second AoE damage, with a final burst after 4.5 seconds or can be activated by a final press of E. These 2 rings can be placed on top one another to make a larger ring with 2x range and 1.5x damage. Cinnamon Bun can run into the ring to get a 20% buff to attack speed and attack damage.

Cooldown: 55s (42s at level 10)
