Princess Bubblegum
A science genius, and the ruler of the Candy Kingdom.
Release date 19-03-2014
Attack range type Ranged
Roles Trapper, Jinxer

Abilities

Sticky Sweet (Passive)

Princess Bubblegum begins with 3 charges of her special sticky ammunition. Each basic attack she lands on enemy players consumes a charge, reducing their attack speed by 20% for 5 seconds per charge. This debuff stacks, allowing for a maximum reduction of 60% if all three charges are applied as well as a 25% slow for 2.5 seconds (if applied to the same target). Every 10 seconds, Princess Bubblegum automatically regenerates one charge of sticky ammunition.

Cooldown: none

Gumdrop Potion (Q)

Princess Bubblegum lobs a scientific potion at a target location, creating a puddle that does damage (5 + 50% power damage) per second and slows foes in the area by 30% for 2 seconds. Entering the area grants Princess Bubblegum a 40% speed boost for 2 seconds. The puddle lasts for 3 seconds.

Cooldown: 10s (8s at level 10)

Candy Turrets (W)

Princess Bubblegum drops candy-powered turrets (2 max) that blasts enemies (10 + 40% power damage), prioritizing enemy champions. Each Candy Turret lasts for 30 seconds or until it is destroyed or Princess Bubblegum is KO'ed. Candy Turrets' health scale with Princess Bubblegum's level (+ 50 health per level).

Cooldown: 20s (16s at level 10)

Cherry Boom (E)

Princess Bubblegum plants a bomb that she can trigger by pressing E again or will explode on its own after 4 seconds. When set off, an explosive blast will damage (85 + 80% power damage) and knockback enemies away from the bomb. Bomb does 25% more damage to slowed enemies. If PB is in the area, the knockback will also affect her.

Cooldown: 55s (40s at level 10)
