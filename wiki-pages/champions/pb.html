Abilities

Sticky Sweet Passive

Princess Bubblegum begins with 3 charges of her special sticky ammunition. Each basic attack she lands on enemy players consumes a charge, reducing their attack speed by 20% for 5 seconds per charge.

This debuff stacks, allowing for a maximum reduction of 60% if all three charges are applied to the same target. It also applies a 25% slow for 2.5 seconds. Every 10 seconds, Princess Bubblegum automatically regenerates one charge of sticky ammunition.

Cooldown: none

Gumdrop Potion Q

Princess Bubblegum lobs a scientific potion at a target location, creating a puddle that deals 5 + 50% power damage per second and slows foes in the area by 30% for 2 seconds.

Entering the area grants Princess Bubblegum a 40% speed boost for 2 seconds. The puddle lasts for 3 seconds.

Cooldown: 10s, 8s at level 10

Candy Turrets W

Princess Bubblegum drops candy-powered turrets, up to 2 max, that blast enemies for 10 + 40% power damage, prioritizing enemy champions.

Each Candy Turret lasts for 30 seconds or until it is destroyed or Princess Bubblegum is KO'ed. Candy Turrets' health scales with Princess Bubblegum's level by +50 health per level and 20% of HP gained from items.

Cooldown: 20s, 16s at level 10

Cherry Boom E

Princess Bubblegum plants a bomb that she can trigger by pressing E again, or it will explode on its own after 4 seconds.

When set off, the explosive blast deals 85 + 60% power damage and knocks back enemies away from the bomb. The bomb deals 25% more damage to slowed enemies. If Princess Bubblegum is in the area, the knockback also affects her.

Cooldown: 65s, 60s at level 10
