Abilities

Snarl Passive

Jake's basic attacks root opponents for 1 second and deal bonus attack damage +60%.

This can only happen once every 8 seconds to a given target. Snarl bonus damage does not affect structures.

Cooldown: 8s

Stretchy Grab Q

Jake extends his arms to incredible lengths, damaging the first enemy hit for 35 + up to 135% power damage and stunning them for 2 seconds.

The farther the enemy, the greater the damage. Scaling increases linearly across the entire range. Jake then rapidly pulls himself to that enemy, ignoring intervening obstacles and setting himself up for another attack.

Cooldown: 12s, 8s at level 10

Jake Balloon W

Jake inflates himself into the form of a huge ball, knocking back and damaging nearby foes for 10 + 60% power damage.

Cooldown: 14s, 12s at level 10

Giant Jake E

Jake grows huge and stomps around for 5 seconds, damaging nearby foes for 65 + 70% power damage per second when he moves.

While active, Jake cannot basic attack or use other powers, but he is immune to negative status effects except knockback. Activating the power removes existing roots or slows.

While active, Jake's speed is decreased by 20%.

Cooldown: 60s, 45s at level 10
