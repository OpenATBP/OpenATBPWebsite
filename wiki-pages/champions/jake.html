Jake
A totally off-the-hook party dog!
Release date 19-03-2014
Attack range type Melee
Roles Tank, Jinxer

Abilities

Snarl (Passive)

Jake's basic attacks root opponents for 1 second and do bonus attack damage +60%. This can only happen once every 8 seconds to a given target. Snarl bonus damage does not affect structures (Towers and Bases).

Cooldown: 10s

Stretchy Grab (Q)

Jake extends his arms to incredible lengths with his Stretchy Grab, damaging (35 + up to 130% power damage) and stuns the first enemy he hits for 2 seconds. Scaling depends on distance, with enemy's further away taking more damage. He will then rapidly pull himself to that enemy, ignoring intervening obstacles and setting himself up for another attack.

Cooldown: 12s (8s at level 10)

Jake Balloon (W)

Jake inflates himself into the form of a huge ball, knocking back and damaging (65 + 60% power damage) all nearby foes.

Cooldown: 14s (12s at level 10)

Giant Jake (E)

Jake grows huge and stomps around for 5 seconds, damaging (100 + 70% power damage) per second nearby foes when he moves. While the ability is active, Jake cannot perform basic attacks or use other powers, but he is immune to negative status effects (except knockback) and will remove any existing roots or slows when the power is activated. When this ability is active, Jake's speed is decreased by 20%.

Cooldown: 60s (45s at level 10)
