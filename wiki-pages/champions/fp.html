Flame Princess
The chaotic ruler of the Fire Kingdom.
Release date 19-03-2014
Attack range type Ranged
Roles Wizard, Ninja

Abilities

Scatter Fire (Passive)

After Flame Princess hits an opponent with a power, her next basic attack will set her opponent on fire (10 + 45% power damage) per second for 3 seconds.

Cooldown: 10s

Flame Blast (Q)

Flame Princess throws a fiery projectile that does (25 + 50% power damage) on hit and bursts into a fan of flames behind the target, doing 120% of the initial damage to enemies in the area. Resets auto-attack timer.

Cooldown: 12s (8s at level 10)

Flambit Polymorph (W)

Flame Princess calls down fire onto a targeted area, damaging (50 + 30% power damage) any enemies caught in the blast. Enemy Champions hit are morphed into Flambits for 3 seconds. Flambits can't attack or use powers and are slowed by 15% for 3 seconds, but can still move around and will damage (50 + 30% power damage) per second their nearby allies.

Cooldown: 20s (15s at level 10)

Blazing Inferno (E)

Flame Princess transforms into a Blazing Inferno doing massive damage (75 + 50% power damage) per second to nearby foes. In this form, she can move, attack and use other powers and can also dash up to 3 times by pressing E again. Blazing Inferno will last 5 seconds or until the 3rd dash is used, whichever comes first.

Cooldown: 60s (45s at level 10)
