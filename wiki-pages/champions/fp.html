Abilities

Scatter Fire Passive

After Flame Princess hits an opponent with a power, her next basic attack sets the opponent on fire, dealing 10 + 25% power damage per second for 3 seconds.

Cooldown: 10s

Flame Blast Q

Flame Princess throws a fiery projectile that deals 10 + 65% power damage on hit and bursts into a fan of flames behind the target.

The fan of flames deals 50% of the initial damage to enemies in the area. This ability resets the auto-attack timer.

Cooldown: 12s, 8s at level 10

Flambit Polymorph W

Flame Princess calls down fire onto a targeted area, dealing 50 + 50% power damage to enemies caught in the blast.

Enemy champions hit are morphed into Flambits for 3 seconds. Flambits cannot attack or use powers and are slowed by 15%, but they can still move around.

Flambits damage nearby allies for 50 + 30% power damage per second.

Cooldown: 20s, 15s at level 10

Blazing Inferno E

Flame Princess transforms into a Blazing Inferno, dealing massive damage: 35 + 55% power damage per second to nearby foes.

In this form, she can move, attack, and use other powers. She can also dash up to 3 times by pressing E again.

Blazing Inferno lasts 5 seconds or until the 3rd dash is used, whichever comes first.

Cooldown: 68s, 60s at level 10
