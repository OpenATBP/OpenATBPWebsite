Peppermint Butler
Princess Bubblegum's loyal servant.
Release date 19-03-2014
Attack range type Melee
Roles Fighter, Jinxer

Abilities

Burrowing (Passive)

Peppermint Butler can stand still for 1.75 seconds to go stealth. The power lasts until he moves, attacks, or takes damage. He heals while stealthed (2% health per second). Peppermint Butler is immune to crowd control for 2 seconds when exiting his passive.

Cooldown: none

Minty Aura (Q)

Peppermint Butler exudes a dense, minty fog for 5 seconds that damages (20 + 20% power damage) per second and blinds any foes in its area.

Cooldown: 15s (13s at level 10)

You Rang? (W)

Peppermint Butler dives underground and quickly tunnels to his target location; exploding upward, he damages (50 + 50% power damage) and stuns nearby foes for 1.5 seconds. Refund up to 20% cooldown based on how long you are stationary.

Cooldown: 15s (13s at level 10)

Sense Evil (E)

Peppermint Butler uses dark magic to reveal hidden foes and becomes a feral fighting machine for 7 seconds. He gains increased speed +40%, attack speed +30%, attack damage +30% and +0.1 attack range boost.

Cooldown: 40s (25s at level 10)
