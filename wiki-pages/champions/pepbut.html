Abilities

Burrowing Passive

Peppermint Butler can stand still for 1.75 seconds to go stealth. The power lasts until he moves, attacks, or takes damage.

He heals while stealthed for 2% health per second. Peppermint Butler is immune to crowd control for 2 seconds when exiting his passive.

Cooldown: none

Minty Aura Q

Peppermint Butler exudes a dense, minty fog for 5 seconds that deals 20 + 20% power damage per second and blinds any foes in its area.

Cooldown: 15s, 13s at level 10

You Rang? W

Peppermint Butler dives underground and quickly tunnels to his target location. When he explodes upward, he deals 50 + 50% power damage and stuns nearby foes for 1.5 seconds.

Refunds up to 20% cooldown based on how long you are stationary. If used while Sense Evil is active, the stun duration is increased to 2 seconds.

Cooldown: 15s, 13s at level 10

Sense Evil E

Peppermint Butler uses dark magic to reveal hidden foes and becomes a feral fighting machine for 7 seconds.

He gains +25% speed, +30% attack speed, +30% attack damage, and +0.5 attack range.

Cooldown: 50s, 40s at level 10
