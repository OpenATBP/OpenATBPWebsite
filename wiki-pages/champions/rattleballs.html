Sir Rattleballs
The shadowy protecter of the Candy Kingdom.
Release date 19-03-2014
Attack range type Melee
Roles Fighter, Ninja

Abilities

Dodge, Parry (Passive)

For 3 seconds after using an ability, Rattleballs' next 2 basic attacks will heal him for 65% of the damage he deals. Dodge, Parry does not activate on structures (Towers and Bases).

Cooldown: none

Shadowless Thrust (Q)

Rattleballs dashes into Counter Stance. If hit by a basic attack he will Counter Attack, taking no damage and dealing a crit attack to his attacker. If not attacked for 1.5 seconds, he will Spin Attack dealing (75 + 50% power damage) to nearby foes. Press Q again to Dash Attack damaging (50 + 50% power damage) foes in his path.

Cooldown: 12s (10s at level 10)

Dust Storm (W)

Rattleballs' awesome sword powers deal damage (75 + 40% power damage) in a large area and pull any opponents within range toward him.

Cooldown: 16s (14s at level 10)

The Sword Stuff (E)

Rattleballs pulls a blade spinning move that repeatedly damages all nearby foes (70 + 70% power damage) per second and gaining a small increase to his own speed while active +14% for 3.5 seconds. He can press E again to cancel the power early. During the duration, all enemy skill shots are destroyed if it enters the sword radius. The Sword Stuff speed boost is not affected by other movement speed increases.

Cooldown: 60s (50s at level 10)
