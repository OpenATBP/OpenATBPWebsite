Abilities

Dodge, Parry Passive

For 3 seconds after using an ability, Rattleballs' next 2 basic attacks heal him for 65% of the damage he deals.

Dodge, Parry does not activate on structures.

Cooldown: none

Shadowless Thrust Q

Rattleballs dashes into Counter Stance. If hit by a basic attack, he will Counter Attack, taking no damage and dealing 100% of his attack damage and 50% of his power damage back to the attacker.

If not attacked for 1.5 seconds, he will Spin Attack, dealing 75 + 50% power damage to nearby foes. Press Q again to Dash Attack, damaging 20 + 50% power damage to foes in his path.

Cooldown: 12s, 10s at level 10

Dust Storm W

Rattleballs' awesome sword powers deal 20 + 65% power damage in a large area and pull any opponents within range toward him.

Cooldown: 16s, 14s at level 10

The Sword Stuff E

Rattleballs pulls a blade spinning move that repeatedly damages all nearby foes for 70 + 70% power damage per second. While active, he gains +14% speed for 3.5 seconds.

He can press E again to cancel the power early. While active, all enemy non-ultimate skillshots that enter the sword's radius are destroyed. The Sword Stuff speed boost is not affected by other movement speed increases.

Cooldown: 60s, 50s at level 10
