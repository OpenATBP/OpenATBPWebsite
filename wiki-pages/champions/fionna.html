Abilities

Tenacious Passive

Fionna does more attack damage and power damage as her health drops.

The less HP she has, the more damage she does, up to a 60% bonus when at 1% health. Tenacious bonus damage does not affect structures, such as towers and bases.

Cooldown: none

Blade Rush Q

Fionna dashes forward, slashing through enemies with Blade Rush. This ability can be used up to three times in quick succession before going on cooldown.

Each dash can be directed in a different direction, dealing 20 + 40% power damage to enemies. The first two dashes hit a small area, while the final dash strikes a larger area and slows enemies by 50% for 1 second.

If Fionna does not hit anything with any of the dashes, Blade Rush's cooldown is reduced by 30%.

Cooldown: 14s, 12s at level 10

Fierce / Fearless W

Fionna can toggle between Fierce and Fearless.

Fierce slowly drains Fionna's health by 2% per second, but cannot reduce her below 1 health. While active, it boosts her speed and attack speed by 20%.

Fearless slowly heals Fionna by 1% per second while granting +30% armor and shields.

Cooldown: 5s at all levels

Invincible! E

For 5 seconds, Fionna cannot be KO'ed. She can still take damage and suffer other effects, but she cannot go below 1 health.

Cooldown: 90s, 60s at level 10
