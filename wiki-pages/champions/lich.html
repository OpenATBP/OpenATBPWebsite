Abilities

Undead Servant Passive

When Undead Servant is ready, any power used by The Lich summons Skully for 20 seconds.

Skully follows The Lich and attacks his targets, dealing 50% spell damage. Any enemies Skully defeats permanently increase The Lich's power damage by 1.

Skully moves at 2.75 speed and returns to The Lich if he travels too far.

Cooldown: 40s

Trail of Death Q

The Lich leaves an unholy trail that persists for 4 seconds, dealing 20 + 30% power damage per second and slowing enemies caught in the affected area by 30% for 1.5 seconds.

Enemies KO'd by the trail permanently increase The Lich's power damage by 1. The spell boosts The Lich's speed by 25% while active.

Cooldown: 12s, 10.5s at level 10

Mind Control W

The Lich sends out a ball of dark magic that damages the first enemy it hits for 40 + 60% power damage and charms them for 2 seconds. Enemies KO'ed by Mind Control permanently increase Lich's Power Damage by 1

Cooldown: 15s, 10s at level 10

Well of Power E

The Lich conjures a Well of Power that persists for 5 seconds and deals 50 + 50% power damage per second to enemies caught in the area. Enemies KO'ed by Well of Power permanently increase Lich's Power Damage by 1.

The Lich can press E again to teleport to the center of Well of Power.

Cooldown: 75s, 45s at level 10
