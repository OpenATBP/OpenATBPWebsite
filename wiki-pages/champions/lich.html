The Lich
The ultimate evil in the Land of Ooo.
Release date 19-03-2014
Attack range type Ranged
Roles Wizard, Trapper
Statistics per level

Abilities

Undead Servant (Passive)

When Undead Servant is ready, any power used by Lich summons Skully for 20s. Skully will follow the Lich and attack his targets dealing (10 + 40% spell damage). Any enemies Skully defeats permanently increase Lich's power damage by 1. Skully moves at 2.75 speed. Skully will return to Lich if he travels too far.

Cooldown: 40s

Trail of Death (Q)

The Lich leaves an unholy trail that persists for 4 seconds dealing (20 + 20% power damage) per second and slows any enemies that get caught in the affected area by 30% for 1.5 seconds. Enemies KO'd by the trail permanently increase Lich's power damage by 1. The spell boosts Lich's speed by 25% while active.

Cooldown: 12s (10.5s at level 10)

Mind Control (W)

The Lich sends out a ball of dark magic that damages (40 + 40% power damage) and charms the first enemy it encounters for 2 seconds.

Cooldown: 15s (10s at level 10)

Well of Power (E)

The Lich conjures a Well of Power that persists for 5 seconds and deals damage (50 + 50% power damage) per second to enemies caught in the area. Lich can press E again to teleport to the center of Well of Power.

Cooldown: 75s (45s at level 10)
