Abilities

Fury Passive

Finn harnesses the complex emotions of a human boy. Hitting the same enemy multiple times with basic attacks, Bombastic Dash, or The Ring! increases Finn's damage to that enemy by 20%, stacking up to 3 times for a total of 60% increased damage.

Hitting a different enemy resets Finn's Fury.

Cooldown: none

Armored Attack Q

Finn creates a shield for 3 seconds that resets his auto-attack timer and boosts his armor by 15% and attack speed by 20%.

If Finn reaches max Fury while Armored Attack is active, the shield explodes, dealing 20 + 60% power damage. The shield also explodes if Finn is KO'ed while it is active.

While the shield is active, Finn also gains a +0.5 speed boost.

Cooldown: 10s, 8s at level 10

Bombastic Dash W

Finn dashes forward and slashes enemies in his path, dealing 20 + 20% power damage.

Bombastic Dash adds Fury, and its cooldown refreshes on kills and assists.

Cooldown: 12s, 10s at level 10

The Ring! E

Finn drops 4 sword walls, creating a fighting ring around himself. The ring damages enemies for 65 + 50% power damage and roots the first enemy to touch each wall for 2 seconds.

The Ring! adds Fury.

Cooldown: 40s, 26s at level 10
