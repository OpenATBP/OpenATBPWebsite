Finn
A heroic human boy with a heart of gold.
Release date 19-03-2014
Attack range type Melee
Roles Fighter, Ninja

Abilities

Fury (Passive)

Finn harnesses the complex emotions of a human boy! Hitting the same enemy multiple times with basic attacks, Bombastic Dash or The Ring! will increase Finn's damage to that enemy by 20% up to 3 times (for a total of 60% damage). Hitting a different enemy will reset Finn's Fury.

Cooldown: none

Armored Attack (Q)

Finn creates a shield for 3 seconds that resets his auto-attack timer and boosts his armor by 25% and attack speed by 20%. If Finn reaches max Fury while Armored Attack is active, the shield explodes, doing damage (50 + 60% power damage). The shield also explodes if Finn is KO'ed while it is active. Also, as long as the shield is active, finn gets a +0.5 speed boost.

Cooldown: 10s (8s at level 10)

Bombastic Dash (W)

Finn dashes forward and slashes enemies in his path (75 + 20% power damage). Bombastic Dash adds Fury and it's cooldown refreshes on kills and assists.

Cooldown: 9s (7s at level 10)

The Ring! (E)

Finn drops 4 sword walls creating a fighting ring around himself. The ring damages (65 + 20% power damage) and roots for 2 seconds the first enemy to touch each wall. The Ring! adds Fury.

Cooldown: 40s (26s at level 10)
