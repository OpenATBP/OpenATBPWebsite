Abilities

Chase Passive

When Magic Man hits other Champions with a basic attack, he gets a 20% speed boost for 3 seconds.

Cooldown: none

Energy Snakes Q

Magic Man releases 3 energy snake projectiles that damage 20 + 70% power damage each and silence opponents for 2 seconds. This ability resets the auto-attack timer.

Subsequent snakes deal 75% less damage to the same target.

Cooldown: 10s, 8s at level 10

EAT IT! W

Magic Man teleports to a target location and goes stealth for 3 seconds or until revealed, leaving behind a decoy that will explode after a delay.

The decoy deals 50 + 70% power damage to nearby enemies. Magic Man can detonate the decoy early by pressing W again.

Cooldown: 25s, 19s at level 10

Magic Away E

Magic Man rolls to a target location, ignoring obstacles, and explodes, dealing 100 + 50% power damage.

Any enemies affected suffer a 20% debuff to armor and shields for 3 seconds and are also slowed by 20% for 2.5 seconds.

Cooldown: 60s, 40s at level 10
