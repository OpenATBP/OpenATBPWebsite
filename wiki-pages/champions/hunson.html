Abilities

Karate Passive

Auto-attacking a target with any status effect grants 50% attack speed and +1 speed for 3.5 seconds.

Hunson starts with 20% power vamp. Karate activation resets auto-attack delay.

Cooldown: 1.5 seconds

Evil Hand Q

Over the next 4.5 seconds, Hunson can throw up to 3 projectiles that pull opponents and slow them by 10% for 2 seconds.

Each projectile deals 25 + 50% power damage. Multiple hits against the same target deal 100% / 100% / 125% damage.

Auto-attacks and powers can be used between casts.

Cooldown: 12s, 8s at level 10

Fear Face W

Hunson exposes his demon face, damaging nearby foes for 20 + 40% power damage per second.

Enemies hit are feared for 1.5 seconds.

Cooldown: 16s, 12s at level 10

Soul Suck E

Hunson creates a large AoE that deals 100 + 60% power damage per second for 3.5 seconds.

Hunson gains +50% armor and +45% power vamp for the duration of the power.

Hunson can move slowly while casting Soul Suck.

Cooldown: 65s, 45s at level 10
