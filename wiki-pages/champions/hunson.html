Hunson Abadeer
Marceline's dad, ruler of the Nightosphere.
Release date 2015-04-13
Attack range type Melee
Roles Wizard, Fighter

Abilities

Karate (Passive)

Auto-attacking a target with any status effect grants 50% attack speed and +1 speed buff for 3.5 seconds. Hunson starts with 20% power vamp. Karate activation resets auto-attack delay.

Cooldown: 5 seconds

Evil Hand (Q)

Over the next 4.5 seconds, throw up to 3 projectiles that pull opponents and slow by 10% for 2 seconds, dealing (25 + 50% power damage). Multiple hits against the same target deals 100%/30%/10% damage. Auto-attacks and powers can be used between casts.

Cooldown: 12s (8s at level 10)

Fear Face (W)

Hunson exposes his demon face damaging (20 + 30% power damage) per second and fearing all nearby foes for 1.5 seconds.

Cooldown: 16s (12s at level 10)

Soul Suck (E)

Large AoE dealing (100 + 50% power damage) per second for 3.5 seconds. Hunson gains +50% armor and +45% power vamp buff for the duration of the power. Hunson can move slowly while casting Soul Suck.

Cooldown: 50s (35s at level 10)
