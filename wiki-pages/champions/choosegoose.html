Choose Goose
A rhyming merchant with magical wares to share.
Release date 31-05-2025
Attack range type Melee
Roles Fighter, Jinxer
Statistics per level

Abilities

Treasure Hoarder (Passive)

Whenever Choose Goose scores a kill or assist on an enemy champion, a treasure chest spawns at the target’s death location for 10 seconds. If collected by Choose Goose or an ally, the collector is instantly healed for 5% of their maximum health and gains +20% movement speed and +20% armor for 6 seconds. Chest spawns have a 20-second cooldown. Each collected chest permanently grants Choose Goose +5 maximum health.

Cooldown: 20s

Electric Charge (Q)

Choose Goose charges his hands with electricity, empowering his basic attacks for 5 seconds. While this charge persists, Choose Goose gains +20% attack speed and +10% attack damage. His first empowered auto-attack against each unique enemy shocks them, dealing (20 + 20% power damage) per second over 3 seconds. If an enemy is struck three times by these empowered attacks during the effect, their armor is reduced by 20% for 3 seconds. Activating this ability resets the auto attack timer.

Cooldown: 11s (8s at level 10)

Goose Leap (W)

Choose Goose leaps to a target location, crashing down to deal (50 + 40% power damage) to enemies in the impact zone. Enemies caught at the epicenter of the impact are silenced for 1 second. Scoring a kill with Goose Leap instantly resets its cooldown.

Cooldown: 15s (12s at level 10)

Cursebreaker (E)

Choose Goose swings his purple axe, releasing a magical projectile that explodes on the first enemy (player, jungle boss or nexus) dealing (190 + 70% power damage) and slowing them by 40%. If the projectile hits an enemy, Choose Goose gains a magical shield for 2 seconds, increasing his armor by 20% and granting him immunity to crowd control.

Cooldown: 55s (42s at level 10)
