Abilities

Treasure Hoarder Passive

Whenever Choose Goose scores a kill or assist on an enemy champion, a treasure chest spawns at the target's death location for 10 seconds.

If collected by Choose Goose or an ally, the collector is instantly healed for 25% of their maximum health and gains +20% movement speed and +20% armor for 6 seconds.

Chest spawns have a 20-second cooldown. Each collected chest permanently grants Choose Goose +10 maximum health.

Cooldown: 20s

Electric Charge Q

Choose Goose charges his hands with electricity, empowering his basic attacks for 5 seconds. While this charge persists, Choose Goose gains +20% attack speed and +10% attack damage.

His first empowered auto-attack against each unique enemy shocks them, dealing 20 + 20% power damage per second over 3 seconds. If an enemy is struck three times by these empowered attacks during the effect, their armor is reduced by 20% for 3 seconds.

Activating this ability resets the auto attack timer.

Cooldown: 11s, 8s at level 10

Goose Leap W

Choose Goose leaps to a target location, crashing down to deal 50 + 40% power damage to enemies in the impact zone.

Enemies caught at the epicenter of the impact are silenced for 1 second. Upon impact, Choose Goose gains a magical shield for 2.5 seconds, increasing his armor by 30% and granting immunity to crowd control during this time.

Cooldown: 15s, 12s at level 10

Hexlash E

Choose Goose swings his purple axe, launching a magical projectile that explodes on the first enemy hit, dealing 190 + 70% power damage.

The target is rooted for 2 seconds. This can hit champions, jungle bosses, or the Nexus.

Cooldown: 55s, 42s at level 10
