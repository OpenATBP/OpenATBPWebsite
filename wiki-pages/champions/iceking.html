Ice King
The king of ice and snow, aka Simon Petrikov.
Release date 19-03-2014
Attack range type Ranged
Roles Wizard, Trapper

Abilities

Ice Shield (Passive)

After 10 seconds of not using a power, Ice King will create an Ice Shield around himself. When active, the next Champion to attack Ice King will break the Ice Shield. If the Ice Shield is broken by a basic attack, the damage is reduced by 50% and the attacker will be slowed by 25% for 2 seconds plus suffer a 33% attack speed penalty for 2 seconds. Ice King deals 10% more damage to crowd controlled enemies.

Cooldown: 5s

Deep Freeze (Q)

Ice King fires an icy projectile that will damage (10 + 25% power damage) the first enemy it hits and encase them in chilling ice shards that root for 1.75s and deal (10 + 25% power damage) over time.

Cooldown: 10s (7s at level 10)

Hailstorm (W)

With a flap of his beard, Ice King bombards an area with gigantic hailstones, damaging (30 + 30% power damage) per second any foes caught in the targeted area.

Cooldown: 12s (8s at level 10)

Frosty Doom (E)

Ice King creates a huge ice patch that damages (30 + 20% power damage) per second enemies caught in the area, and boosts Ice King's speed +90%.

Cooldown: 60s (45s at level 10)
