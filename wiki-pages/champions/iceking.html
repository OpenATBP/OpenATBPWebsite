Abilities

Ice Shield Passive

After 10 seconds of not using a power, Ice King creates an Ice Shield around himself. When active, the next champion to attack Ice King breaks the Ice Shield.

If the Ice Shield is broken by a basic attack, the damage is reduced by 50%. The attacker is slowed by 25% for 2 seconds and suffers a 33% attack speed penalty for 2 seconds.

Ice King deals 10% more damage to crowd controlled enemies.

Cooldown: 5s

Deep Freeze Q

Ice King fires an icy projectile that damages the first enemy it hits for 10 + 25% power damage.

The target is encased in chilling ice shards that root for 1.75 seconds and deal 10 + 25% power damage over time.

Cooldown: 12s, 9s at level 10

Hailstorm W

With a flap of his beard, Ice King bombards an area with gigantic hailstones, damaging enemies caught in the target area for 30 + 35% power damage per second.

Cooldown: 14s, 10s at level 10

Frosty Doom E

Ice King creates a huge ice patch that damages enemies caught in the area for 30 + 35% power damage per second.

While active, Ice King gains +90% speed.

Cooldown: 70s, 60s at level 10
