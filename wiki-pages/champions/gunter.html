Abilities

Shatter Passive

When Gunter KOs an enemy with a basic attack or Wing It, a shower of broken glass damages nearby foes for 10 + 100% power damage.

Cooldown: none

Belly Slide Q

Gunter dashes to a targeted location with Belly Slide, ignoring any obstacles and exploding in a burst of bottles at his destination.

The explosion damages nearby enemies for 20 + 65% power damage.

Cooldown: 12s, 9s at level 10

Wing It W

Gunter hurls a gigantic bottle projectile which damages the first enemy it hits for 20 + 75% power damage.

If Wing It lands a knock-out blow, it triggers Shatter.

Cooldown: 7.5s, 5s at level 10

Bottle Barrage E

Gunter unleashes a steady onslaught of bottles, dealing 60 + 70% power damage per second to all enemies in a large area.

Gunter can cancel Bottle Barrage early by using Belly Slide.

Cooldown: 60s, 42s at level 10
