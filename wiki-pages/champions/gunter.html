Gunter
The Ice King's right-hand penguin.
Release date 19-03-2014
Attack range type Ranged
Roles Ninja

Abilities

Shatter (Passive)

When Gunter KOs an enemy with a basic attack or Wing It, a shower of broken glass damages (10 + 100% power damage) nearby foes.

Cooldown: none

Belly Slide (Q)

Gunter dashes to a targeted location with Belly Slide, ignoring any obstacles and exploding in a burst of bottles at his destination, damaging (50 + 50% power damage) nearby enemies.

Cooldown: 12s (9s at level 10)

Wing It (W)

Gunter hurls an gigantic bottle projectile which will damage (50 + 60% power damage) the first enemy it hits. If Wing It lands a knock-out blow it will trigger Shatter.

Cooldown: 7.5s (5s at level 10)

Bottle Barrage (E)

Gunter unleashes a steady onslaught of bottles, doing (60 + 70% power damage) per second to all enemies in a large area. Gunter can cancel Bottle Barrage early by using Belly Slide.

Cooldown: 60s (42s at level 10)
