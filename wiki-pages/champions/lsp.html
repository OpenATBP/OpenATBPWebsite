Abilities

My Lumps Passive

LSP's basic attacks add Lumps, up to 10 max. Lumps are consumed by Buuuumps to heal 3% of her max health for each Lump she has accumulated, up to 30%.

Cooldown: none

Buuuumps Q

Lumpy Space Princess fires a lumpy beam blast that damages 10 + 70% power damage and fears all enemies it hits for 2 seconds.

Buuuumps also heals Lumpy Space Princess for a percentage of her current maximum health based on My Lumps.

Cooldown: 15s, 12s at level 10

Hot Bod W

Lumpy Space Princess forms a super hot cloud around her bod that persists for 4 seconds and moves with her dealing 20 + 50% power damage per second to enemies caught inside.

Cooldown: 12s, 7s at level 10

Drama Bomb E

Lumpy Space Princess winds up and throws her phone across the entire map. It hurts enemies and heals allies in its path for 155 + 70% power damage.

The amount is reduced by 30% on each subsequent target.

Cooldown: 75s, 30s at level 10
