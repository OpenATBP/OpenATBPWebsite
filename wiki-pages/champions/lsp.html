Lumpy Space Princess
Princess of Lumpy Space.
Release date 19-03-2014
Attack range type Ranged
Roles Wizard

Abilities

My Lumps (Passive)

LSP's basic attacks add Lumps (10 max). Lumps are consumed by Buuuumps to heal 3% of her max health for each Lump she has accumulated, up to 30%.

Cooldown: none

Buuuumps (Q)

Lumpy Space Princess fires a lumpy beam blast that damages (10 + 70% power damage) and fears all enemies it hits for 2 seconds. Buuuumps also heals Lumpy Space Princess for a percentage of her current maximum health based on My Lumps.

Cooldown: 15s (12s at level 10)

Hot Bod (W)

Lumpy Space Princess forms a super hot cloud around her bod that moves with her and damages (20 + 50% power damage) per second enemies caught inside.

Cooldown: 12s (7s at level 10)

Drama Bomb (E)

Lumpy Space Princess winds up and throws her phone across the entire map. It hurts enemies and heals allies in its path (250 + 40% power damage), the amount is reduced by 30% on each subsequent target.

Cooldown: 75s (30s at level 10)
