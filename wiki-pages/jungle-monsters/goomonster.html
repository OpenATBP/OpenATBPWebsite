Goo Monster

The Goo Monster is a jungle boss found at the top of Battle Lab. Upon being killed, it grants 25 points and 10% speed buff that lasts 60 seconds. The Goo Monster spawns initially at 13:30 and respawns every 90 seconds after being killed.

Goo Monster
Health 1750
Armor 10
Shields 10
Attack damage 50
Attack speed 1350
Attack range 3
Speed 1.5
Score value 25

Ability

Slimed

The Goo Monster summons an ooze pool that persists for 3 seconds and deals 40 power damage per second to all champions located in the ability area and also slows them by 25% for 1 second.

Cooldown: 5 seconds
