Kee-Oth

Kee-Oth is the strongest jungle monster in the Battle Lab. Upon being killed, the team that landed the killing blow heals 30% health and get a buff that grants 35% attack vamp, 40% power vamp and 35% critical chance bonus for 60 seconds. Keeoth spawns initially at 13:00 and respawns every 120 seconds after being killed.

Ability

Pyroclasm

After a brief delay (1.15s) a massive fireball drops on the targeted area. Foes caught in the outer ring take 150 power damage while enemies in the center ring take 450 power damage. Damage is unleashed upon them after a brief 1-second interval.

Cooldown: 3 seconds
