About Champions

Champions are the stars of the show in OpenATBP. They are the characters you jump into battle with, each bringing their own style, powers, and personality to the game. Whether you're smashing enemies up close or blasting them from afar, there is a champion for every kind of playstyle.

Every champion comes with three active abilities mapped to Q, W, and E, plus a passive ability that gives them extra flavor. Some passives are always active, while others trigger automatically when specific things happen during the match.

Attack Types

Champions in OpenATBP are categorized by their attack types:

Melee

Champions who fight in close combat and need to stay near their target to attack.

Ranged

Champions who attack from a distance, usually by firing projectiles as basic attacks.
