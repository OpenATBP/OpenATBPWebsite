Champions

Champions are the stars of the show in OpenATBP. They're the characters you jump into battle with, each bringing their own style, powers, and personality to the game. Whether you're smashing enemies up close or blasting them from afar, there's a champ for every kind of playstyle.

Every champion comes with three active abilities (mapped to Q, W, and E) and a passive ability that gives them a little extra flavor. Some passives are always on, while others kick in automatically when certain things happen in the game. The active abilities can do all sorts of things - deal damage, mess with enemy stats, or help your team out in a pinch.

Champions in OpenATBP are categorized by their attack types:

  • Melee: Champions who engage in close combat, requiring physical proximity to their targets to unleash their attacks.
  • Ranged: Characters that attack from a distance, firing projectiles as their basic attacks.

With so many different characters to choose from, every match feels a little different - and that's what keeps the battles exciting!
