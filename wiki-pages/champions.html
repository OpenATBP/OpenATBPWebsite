Champions

Champions form the heart of the action within OpenATBP, representing a diverse array of characters, each equipped with their unique set of skills, abilities, and attributes. These champions serve as the playable characters, offering distinct playstyles and strategies for players seeking to engage in the exhilarating MOBA-style battles.

Each champion in OpenATBP possesses three active abilities and a passive ability, contributing to their distinct playstyle. The Q, W, and E keys allow players to activate a champion's unique skills during gameplay. These abilities vary significantly, ranging from damaging attacks to debuffing opponents or offering beneficial effects to the player's team. Passive abilities may either remain constantly active or trigger automatically based on specific in-game conditions.

Champions in OpenATBP are categorized by their attack types:

  • Melee: Champions who engage in close combat, requiring physical proximity to their targets to unleash their attacks.
  • Ranged: Characters that attack from a distance, firing projectiles as their basic attacks.
